About Me

I work at the Dalle Molle Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IDSIA, USI-SUPSI) in Lugano (Switzerland), where I lead the IDSIA Robotics Lab. Our research focuses on AI for Autonomous Robotics, Machine Learning applications, Human-Robot interaction, Biomedical Image Processing, and Data Visualization.

See my publications on my Google Scholar profile.

Teaching

I teach university courses at the Swiss Master of Science in Engineering (Zurich), USI (Lugano), SUPSI (Lugano), Politecnico di Milano on Robotics, Computer Vision, Deep Learning and Data Science topics.
